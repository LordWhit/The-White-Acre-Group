Build a professional, institutional-quality website for a private holding company named The White Acre Group.

Brand Positioning

The White Acre Group is a long-term business acquisition and operating firm focused on acquiring and stewarding established, profitable small businesses—particularly in industrial, manufacturing, trades, and blue-collar sectors.

The firm prioritizes:

Owner legacy and continuity

Employee retention

Sustainable, long-term operation (not flipping)

Creative, non-SBA deal structures when appropriate

Tone:

Calm

Serious

Trustworthy

Long-term oriented
Not flashy. Not “startup.” Think private equity without arrogance.

Visual Style

Clean, modern, conservative design

Neutral color palette (white, charcoal, deep green or navy)

Serif or professional sans-serif fonts

Minimal animations

Feels like a firm that handles real money and real people

Pages to Build
1. Home

Clear headline about acquiring and operating enduring businesses

Subtext emphasizing legacy, people, and long-term ownership

Call to action:

“Business Owners”

“Investors & Partners”

2. About

Explain the philosophy:

Buying businesses from owners who care about what happens next

Operating with discipline, respect, and patience

Emphasize hands-on ownership and stewardship

No personal bio needed yet (keep it firm-focused)

3. What We Look For

Include criteria such as:

Profitable, established businesses

Strong customer relationships

Simple operations

Owner looking to transition or retire

Preference for industrial, manufacturing, service, or trade businesses

4. Our Approach

Outline:

Flexible deal structures

Seller alignment

Earn-outs, seller participation, gradual transitions

Focus on keeping businesses intact

Avoid technical financing terms. Keep it human.

5. For Business Owners

Reassurance that this is not a broker listing site

Confidential conversations

Respect for employees and customers

Willingness to structure deals creatively

6. Investors & Partners

Long-term capital mindset

Disciplined acquisitions

Focus on downside protection

Opportunities reviewed on a case-by-case basis

No public fundraising language

7. Contact

Simple form

Emphasis on confidentiality

Technical Requirements

Responsive design

Clean HTML/CSS/JS or React

SEO-friendly structure

Placeholder copy that sounds polished and institutional

No fake logos, no fake testimonials

This site should make a broker or seller think:
“This group is real, serious, and built to last.”
